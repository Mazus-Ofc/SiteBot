Convite OAuth2

Exemplo de URL:

https://discord.com/api/oauth2/authorize?client_id=SEU_CLIENT_ID&permissions=8&scope=bot%20applications.commands

Troque SEU_CLIENT_ID pelo ID do app do Discord e ajuste as permissões.
